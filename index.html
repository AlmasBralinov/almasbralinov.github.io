    A+ Education Center

    A+ Education Center—this is a mathematics and physics instructional center for pupils of all levels. I began teaching in the tenth grade, but the center was legally registered in July 2018, when I graduated from high school.

    A + classes are primarily concerned with preparing for admission to physics and mathematical colleges, passing international examinations such as the SAT math or AP calculus, and preparing for math olympiads.

    208 schoolchildren, including myself, were trained in A + Education over four years. When students use the services of related platforms, the learning center is intimately linked with my other projects, such as ScienceWorld.kz with Solve.kz in the past and GoBoston.kz and Integerly.com. A+ Education has been using a distance learning model since March 2020.
